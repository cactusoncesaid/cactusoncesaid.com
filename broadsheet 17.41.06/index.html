For our publication class in LAU, i had to design a broadsheet where i have to choose a long and dense article from a local Arabic newspaper and design it using the information in Arabic only. I experimented between digital and physical, where i printed the text and pasted on it cling film to create more of a suffocated feeling representating what the emotions of the article is protraying.
