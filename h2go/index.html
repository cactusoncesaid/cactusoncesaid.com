H2Go! is a brand that aims to reduce the consumption of bottled water and to encourage a change in behavior. These reusable bottles will be distrubuted throughout campus, focusing on water dispensers so students can refill it as they go. The aim of this project is to create a logo and brand elements. I focused on the elements of earth, as in: water, sun, sky, and land; highlighted in a form of a droplet. The font i have used is fun and buubly, to give friendly and fun sense to it.Part of the brand elments, I have designed the business cards, letterhead, and envelop designs.In addition, I have created a couple of mockups, consiting of: a reusable bottle and a tote bag.
