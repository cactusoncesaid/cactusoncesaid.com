Another project for our publication class, i had to design a publication based on a short story. The goal is to study, understand and analyze the behavior of the characters in the story and how they drive the narrative. Later, we had to reinterpret and adapt the original narrative based on the Lebanese context. I chose the short story called The Murders in the Rue Morgue by Edgar Allan Poe, and renamed it The Murders in Voie 13. I rewrote the story through a designed publication, by displaying the text on transparent acetate paper and the images (i took and edited) printed on calc paper. This publications portrays the mood and atmosphere the story's describing and representing.
