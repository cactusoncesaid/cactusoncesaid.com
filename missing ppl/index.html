In Typography course in LAU, we had to design a typographic publication, meaning a publication with only text; yet, we must portray the emotions of what we are writing about through the typography. My concept is based on the missing people during the civil war in Lebanon. From 1975 till 1990, a war fell upon Lebanon and 17,000 people went missing, leaving an empty place in people’s hearts; especially their loved ones. And these are their testimonies. Here’s a chapter
