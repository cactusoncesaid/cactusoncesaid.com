ENJOYUX/UI DesignHomeAboutPortfolioIn Digital Design Studio, we were assigned to 2 projects throughout the course. One of the projects was based on a brand where we had to redesign it’s application design, by going through the process of testing and reevaluating it’s current features, therefore enhancing. My group was composed of: Chiara Facchini, Laura Guerinoni, Michela Brambilla, and myself. We were assigned the brand Enjoy.During that process, we divided it into 3 phases. First phase, we conducted user tests on the existing design, to have a better understanding as to what is good and bad. First, we conducted the Card sorting text. We divided 43 cards and 8 sections based on the existing app, in which we tested on 20 users: 12 of which have never used Enjoy, 5 of which have used similar services and 2 of which use Enjoy frequently. The results are shown as such:Based on the Card sorting new architecture, we conducted a tree testing by asking the same users 10 different tasks to accomplish, in which we received quantitative and qualitative data. The results are shown as such:As a result from the Card sorting and Tree testing, we finalised the Information Architecture.Based on Information Architecture, we began with the design of the application. We first began with drawing the main structure and elements, then took it to Figma where we designed the main wireframe, then Lo-Fi prototype of the application. Before moving on to the final design, we conducted another user test using the main tasks from the tree testing. Based on those results, we designed the final design of the application. Finally, the last user test was a heat map, in order to make sure all is perfect for the experience of the users.For the final Hi-Fi prototype of the application design, we followed the brand identity of Enjoy, in which includes the brand colours, fonts and much more. Enjoy is an Italian car sharing service that is used among multiple people with a valid driver’s license. It provides cars to rent for the number of time needed to in order to move around all around the major cities of Italy as they please!
