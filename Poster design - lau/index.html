In Poster Design course in LAU, we had to design several sets of posters (3 in each set). Each set was restricted with specific requirements, which falls under: fonts, weight, colors, and images; in order to think outside of the box, even with the least requirements. We also had to mix mediums, experimenting on doing posters by hand, digitally, and both together.
